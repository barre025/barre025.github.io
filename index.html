I am currently a Psychology major, first year transfer at University of California Riverside and third year college student, working towards my masters degree.

I am an Impact Tutor at May Ranch Elementary School in Perris, California. It is one of my greatest joys working at this school. Working with students of all ages is something that I want to continue doing for the rest of my life.

I am a dedicated and compassionate professional with a passion for helping others and a strong commitment to helping children in schools. With not much background in education I have developed a passion for my small job as an Impact tutor. Currently, I am a reading tutor at May Ranch Elementary as stated above, where I engage in small groups with our students that struggle more than others with reading. We as Impact Tutors use a system called 95% which is a semi-scripted daily small workgroup session in which we practice reading and writing skills with the students. This role has allowed me to leverage my skills in communicating with my team effectively while managing a short time frame in order to accomplish the task at hand and reach the goal set. I do believe in the power of teamwork and I love working in collaborative settings such as the one I am in now, where we can share ideas on how to better improve our work ethic. I believe that my attention to detail and ability to follow directions meticulously ensures that every student I meet with is met with the highest standards. Outside of work, I enjoy reading and baking, finding inspiration and relaxation in the pages of a good book and the creativity of the kitchen. This blend of personal interests and professional dedication has made my life all the better and encourages me everyday. I am and will always be eager to connect with others, share insights, and explore new opportunities for growth. With a commitment to continuous learning and a genuine desire to make a difference, I am excited to see where my journey now will lead to in the future.
